General Rules

Respect Others

Treat all players with respect and avoid using offensive or inappropriate language.

No Cheating

Using any programs or tools that give you unfair advantages, such as X-ray or Fly, is prohibited.

Keep It Clean

Avoid placing random blocks or griefing buildings.

Cooperate and Help

Encourage players to work together and assist each other.

Building Rules

Respect Building Areas

Do not build in other players' areas without their permission.

Preserve the Environment

Avoid destroying nature randomly and replant trees after cutting them.

Aesthetic Design

Try to make your designs beautiful and organized.

Resource Rules

Share

Try to distribute resources among players and avoid hoarding.

Conserve

Do not waste resources and use them wisely.

Mining

Make sure not to destroy caves and natural areas while mining.

PvP (Player vs. Player) Rules

Consent

PvP should be consensual; make sure all parties agree before engaging in combat.

Fair Play

Avoid ganging up on single players or spawn camping.

No Griefing

Do not destroy other players' builds or steal their items during or after PvP.
